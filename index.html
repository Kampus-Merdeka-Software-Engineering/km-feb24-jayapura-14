About The Project

This project is our final assignment for the MSIB Independent Study at RevoU TechAcademy. We used our data analysis and web development skills to analyze sales data from Giant Superstore and build an informative website.

We explored the data, identified sales trends, analyzed product profitability, created data visualizations, and made recommendations to boost sales performance.

These insights were then used to design and develop a functional website. This project improved our technical skills and enhanced our ability to communicate information effectively through digital media.

Our Superstore analysis aims to provide a detailed look into the sales data of Giant Superstore. Through data exploration, processing, and visualization, we offer insights for the superstore owner and various stakeholders. From analyzing sales trends to evaluating product profitability, we uncover critical information that can be used to enhance sales performance at the Giant Superstore.
